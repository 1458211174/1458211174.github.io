      港式火鸡包

      港式面包的特点就是用不多的面粉，做出很多又松又软的面包，靠的就是搓和甩使之筋道十足。 Different from western bread, Hongkong style buns are light and fluffy due to the typical way we knead the dough.

      一、材料

      扫面蛋液egg1/2个pc

      温水warmwater130克g

      糖sugar30克g

      酵母yeast5克g

      奶粉milkpowder8克g

      高筋面粉breadflour250克g

      盐salt2克g

      蛋液egg30克g

      牛油butter25克g

      火鸡肉turkeymeat130克g

      玉米粒corn70克g

      黑椒粉blackpepper适量app

      蛋黄酱mayon40g

      二、做法

      步骤
      将酵母水准备好；Mix all ingredients under (A).
      除牛油外，顺次序加入面糊材料；Add all ingredients under (B) except butter.
      如洗衣服般向前推搓数分钟；Knead for few mins.
      搓揉均匀后加入牛油；Add butter after ingredients are well incorporated.
      接下来就是甩，甩得越长越好，做出来的越有筋道；Throw the dough for few mins till elastic.
      加点手粉，搓圆面团，以步骤1的大圆盆盖著，静置30分钟；Flour both hands, form a big round dough, cover with the big pan for 30 mins.
      挤出空气，切割成10个小面团，滚圆，湿布遮盖进行中间发酵15分钟；Press the dough, divide into 10 pcs, make round, cover with wet towel for 15 mins.
      拌匀内陷所有材料；Mix all ingredients under (C).
      把面团擀平，包半月形，最后以叉子压边；Roll out each small dough, wrap like crescent, seal with a fork.
      温暖处发酵30分钟；Rest for another 30 mins.
      涂上一层蛋液；Apply a thin layer of egg.
      每个包上面撒上点香草；Sprinkle some herbs onto the bread surface.
      放进已预热180度的烤箱烤15分钟；Bake for 15 mins at 180C.
